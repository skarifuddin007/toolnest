Troubleshooting & Support

Having issues? Find quick solutions to common problems and technical difficulties.

Common Issues

Quick fixes:

  • Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Try using an incognito/private browsing window
  • Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser

Verification steps:

  • Double-check all input values for accuracy
  • Ensure you're using the correct units (e.g., annual vs monthly rates)
  • Review the calculation method in the tool's help section
  • Compare with manual calculations or other tools
  • Contact us if discrepancies persist

Mobile optimization tips:

  • Rotate your device to landscape mode for better visibility
  • Zoom out if content appears too large
  • Close other browser tabs to free up memory
  • Update your mobile browser to the latest version
  • Try using a different mobile browser

Download troubleshooting:

  • Check if your browser is blocking pop-ups or downloads
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Try right-clicking and "Save As" instead
  • Disable ad blockers temporarily
  • Check your browser's download settings

Privacy & Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. Learn how we protect your information.

We collect minimal data to improve our services: basic usage analytics (which tools are used most), general location data (country level), and technical information (browser type, device type). We do NOT store your calculations, personal information, or any data you input into our tools.

No, absolutely not. All calculations are performed locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers. Your financial data, personal information, and calculation results remain completely private and are automatically cleared when you close your browser or navigate away.

We use minimal, privacy-focused cookies only for essential functionality like remembering your theme preference and basic analytics. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or sell your data to advertisers. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting tool functionality.

Yes, all connections to ToolNest are secured with industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption. Look for the lock icon in your browser's address bar. This ensures that any data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted and protected from interception.

Security Features

All data transmission is protected with 256-bit SSL encryption, the same standard used by banks and financial institutions.

Client-Side Processing

All calculations happen in your browser. Your sensitive data never leaves your device, ensuring maximum privacy.

We don't store your calculations, personal information, or usage patterns. Each session is completely independent and private.

Privacy by Design

Our entire platform is built with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. Minimal data collection, maximum user control.
